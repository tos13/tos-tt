Rules & Management

Match Format

  • Regular Matches: Best of 3 sets (11 pts).
  • Finals Only: Best of 5 sets.
  • Warm-up: Strict 2 minute limit.

Golden Point Rule

If the score reaches 10-10, play a single sudden death point. No "win by 2".
